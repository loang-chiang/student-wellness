Mental health of college students

We aim to raise awareness of the mental health of college students. We reach this idea making a clear visualization of our data and giving free resources to students that may not be able to reach out for help due to a lack of economic support.

Problem

Do you know how is the wellbeing of students in current years? In a recent poll college students have shared a shocking response to questions related to their mental health. The poll shows a declining quality of life in college students that leads to a deteriorating health and daily life.
